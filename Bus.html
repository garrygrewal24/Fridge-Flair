About Fridge Flair

Fridge Flair – Handmade, Eco-Friendly Fridge Magnets

Add a touch of personality to your space with Fridge Flair, where sustainability meets creativity! Our handmade fridge magnets are crafted from recycled coffee grounds and clay, offering a unique, eco-friendly alternative to plastic magnets. Inspired by Kiwi humor, culture, and creativity, each magnet is individually designed, ensuring no two are exactly alike.
